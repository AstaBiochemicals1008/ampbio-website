Who We Are

Amplified Biopharma Solutions

Amplified Biopharma Solutions—Ampbio—provides integrated scientific and biopharma support across specialised product sourcing, scientific consultation, private training and Proof of Concept development.

We support customers from the initial identification of a scientific requirement through material sourcing, technical assessment, research support, validation and next-stage recommendations.

Our Mission

To make advanced biopharma research, therapeutic development and specialised scientific knowledge more accessible through reliable sourcing, expert consultation, confidential training and integrated R&D services.

Our Vision

We aim to remove scientific, sourcing and knowledge barriers that may prevent researchers and therapeutic innovators from advancing important ideas.

What We Support

Our work may include:

  • Specialised reagent and raw-material identification
  • Product and supplier sourcing
  • Scientific literature research
  • Technical-information support
  • Confidential consultation
  • Research and academic support
  • Private scientific training
  • Proof of Concept assistance
  • Review and next-stage recommendations
Confidentiality and Trust

Confidentiality and trust

We understand that scientific, therapeutic and product-development requirements may involve sensitive or non-public information.

Where appropriate, our support may include:
  • Non-Disclosure Agreements
  • Private consultations
  • Restricted-access documentation
  • Controlled sharing of technical information
  • Confidential sourcing requests
  • Private training sessions
  • Non-public research and Proof of Concept projects

All confidential engagements remain subject to applicable ethical, legal and regulatory requirements.

Ampbio is focused on specialised products and services across the biopharma lifecycle, covering a broad portfolio of outsourcing and supply solutions from proof of concept till commercial manufacturing.
