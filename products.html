Requirement-Based Sourcing

Sourcing Begins with the Scientific Requirement

  • Understanding the intended scientific application
  • Relevant technical specifications
  • Identifying suitable products and materials
  • Comparing available options
  • Providing technical information support
  • Conducting confidential sourcing enquiries
  • Supporting reference-product identification
  • Assisting with reference-standard selection

Our Sourcing Process

1
Understand

We review the scientific objective, application and technical requirement.

2
Identify

We identify suitable materials, reagents, products or suppliers.

3
Evaluate

Relevant specifications, technical information and available options are reviewed.

4
Source

The selected product or material is sourced based on the required requirement.

5
Support

Where required, we provide technical information and post-supply scientific support.
